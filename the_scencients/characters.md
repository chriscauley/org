* Kelly Obregon - A former convicted and custodial technician on the Venus mission. Eventually becomes the first superuser.

* Jan Yu - A doll running a fork of Kelly Obregon.

* Unnamed Doll/Amanda Bost/Yua - A badly damaged doll who is assumed dead due to record hacking by Jan. Gets taken over by Jan Yu when she returns from Jupiter (~10 years before the start of the story). After years in a mental hospital she returns to society under the guise of being pure human. At this point she identifies herself as Yua but she is known as Amanda Bost.

* Megan - A human in the low tech ghettors who helps Jan and is eventually helped by Yua.
